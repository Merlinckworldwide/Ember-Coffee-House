Brewing Equipment

Elevate your daily ritual with the tools we use in the shop.

Ember Pro Espresso Machine

Professional-grade temperature control and built-in grinder for the perfect pull.

Tip: Always purge the steam wand before and after texturing milk.

Ceramic Pour-Over Kit

Includes a ceramic dripper and glass carafe for a clean, nuanced cup.

Tip: Rinse the paper filter with hot water before adding grounds to remove any papery taste.

Classic French Press

A simple, elegant way to brew full-bodied coffee without paper filters.

Tip: Use a coarse, even grind and steep for exactly 4 minutes.

Precision Coffee Grinder

Flat burr grinder designed for uniform grind size and maximum flavor extraction.

Tip: Clean the burrs monthly for best performance.

Electric Gooseneck Kettle

Precise temperature control and flow rate for the perfect pour-over experience.

Tip: Aim for 200°F (93°C) for light and medium roasts.

AeroPress Coffee Maker

Compact and durable, perfect for travel or making a quick, clean cup at home.

Tip: Try the inverted method for a longer steeping time.
